About us

Zone Entries are some of the latest research in hockey analytics as shown by research at the latest SSAC. This app is meant to be a single place for all zone entry people to input their zone entry and to share their data. Users can manage their own data and download all zone entry in a convenient .csv file. Further research can be composed with this data as well as data in other databases.

Created by Josh Weissbock with thanks to the feedback of all users.
